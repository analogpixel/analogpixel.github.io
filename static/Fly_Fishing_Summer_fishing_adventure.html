Fly Fishing
Summer fishing adventure
1/9/2016

Many go fishing all their lives without knowing that it is not fish they are after.

  • Henry David Thoreau

How better to decide if you like something that could potentially be a frustrating and expensive first time expereince than to take a guided trip!

Since my friend Anne works for Front Range Anglers in boulder we went with them/her for the trip. It was a half day trip, so we didn't want to spend most of the time traveling, so she picked somewhere really close the Eben G. Fine Park in boulder.

we got there around 7:30 all suited up in wanders and ready to go.

I go fishing not to find myself but to lose myself

  • Joseph Monniger

I'm thinking that fly fishing is just an excuse to stand in the middle of a river and space out a little bit. Sure you have to cast and watch the fly, but that's just a diversion for people walking by so they don't think you are a complete loon for just sitting in the middle of the river spacing out.

The great charm of fly-fishing is that we are always learning

  • Theodore Gordon

Next time I think i'll pack a lunch, and then hike the trail after people start showing up in the morning. with camera and sketchbook in hand, it should make for a delightful day.
